About me

I may not know how to do something at first, but rest assured that I will learn how to do it in short

Hi, I'm Sonia Villa.

After several years working in management positions (first in London and afterwards in Madrid), mainly in development departments, I changed my mind and decided to join the developers crew.

At the beginning, I only used to help out with simple tasks and learn in a self-taught way, but after a while, I started taking training courses to be able to carry out more complex tasks.

Almost fifteen years later, after endless hours in front of the computer, many courses taken and two careers, I work as a web developer in a startup.

I am addicted to reading, to the T.V. series and to chocolate and, in order to relax, I play basketball, go swimming or crochet amigurumis.

A couple of years ago I discovered robotics and also do my first steps with Arduino. Next goal: learn Python.
